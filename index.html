BUT WELL ITS PROLOGUE ABOUT ME :

In the bustling city of Arcadia, a young man known only by his initial, A, lived a life shrouded in mystery. At 19, A possessed a power unlike any other: a dark, sinister force that could bend reality to his will. This power, a malevolent gift, was a burden he bore alone. With coal-black eyes and a costume as dark as the void, A was known to some as The Dark Balance. A’s costume was a sight to behold—black as midnight, with embers of crimson fire dancing along the edges. It cloaked him in shadows, allowing him to move unseen through the city streets. Though his power was formidable, A chose neither to conquer nor to protect. Instead, he walked the line between light and dark, a neutral force in a world of extremes. In this complex existence, A had a partner named Bocchi. Unlike A, Bocchi was firmly on the side of Earth’s defenders. She was brave, compassionate, and always ready to fight for what was right. Despite their different paths, A and Bocchi shared a deep bond, understanding and accepting each other’s roles. One fateful night, an invasion more severe than any before descended upon Arcadia. The sky was torn asunder by alien warships, and the ground quaked under the might of their technology. The city’s defenders, including Bocchi, struggled to hold the line. In the midst of this chaos, A emerged, his presence both feared and revered. He watched as Bocchi and her allies fought valiantly against the invaders. The scales were tipping, and A knew his moment had come. With a wave of his hand, he unleashed his dark power, enveloping a section of the battlefield in impenetrable shadows. Alien technology faltered, their forces thrown into disarray. Bocchi, though surprised, used this advantage to rally the defenders and push back the invaders. After the battle, Bocchi approached A. “Why do you always stay in the shadows, A? You could be a hero.” A’s coal-black eyes met hers. “I am not a hero, Bocchi. Nor am I a villain. I am balance. Without me, the world would tip into chaos.” Bocchi nodded, understanding the heavy burden he carried. “Then we will fight our battles, and you will keep the balance. Together, we’ll protect this world in our own ways.” With a final glance, A vanished into the night, a dark sentinel watching over a world forever teetering on the edge.
